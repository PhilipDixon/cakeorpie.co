New Website

by Naught

Cake or Pie Version 3.0 is live!

It has take a lot of work to get to the stage it is currently at. I have re-written the code for this site several times in order to get it looking how I wanted it. Minimalistic, yet informative.

There is still more work to do however, I am currently planning a content based section where some of our members can contribute to the running of Cake or Pie's ever growing social media presence.

With that in mind, we are currently looking for members of Cake or Pie who stream, create YouTube videos, and have an interest in helping with our Social Media accounts. If you are interested, send an in-game whisper to any of the Guild Masters (Fishy, Naught or Rhandi).

There are still some improvements needed for the website, and not all of the links are active yet, but this coming week will see those open up to show people more about the largest and most active guild on Emerald Dream/Teneras EU.
